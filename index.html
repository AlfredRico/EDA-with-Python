TikTok Claims Project (EDA with Python)

Problem Statement

TikTok's ever growing community base with close to 2 billion monthly users worldwide presents moderators with the challenge of reviewing reported content suspected of containing user claims. In order to maintain trust and safety on the platform, moderators must take precautionary measures to prevent the spread of misinformation and ensure adherence to policy. To help reduce the level of backlogging, the overall focus of this project will be to create a predictive model that can determine whether a video contains claims or offers opinions allowing moderators to prioritize reports efficiently.

Project Goal

For this part of the project, I will be focusing on cursory data inspection of the provided dataset, "tiktok_dataset.csv". Building a dataframe with Python I will then organize the claims data for exploratory data analysis, clean, transform and derive engagement trends identifying any suggestions of correlations between user engagement variables and claim status.
